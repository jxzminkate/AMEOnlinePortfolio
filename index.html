About Me

Hi! I'm Jazmin Kate Aizpuru. I am a 16 year old from the Philippines who loves art,books, traveling, and adventure. And dogs.I spend my free time drawing and reading books. I'm really into horror and thriller books, or anything that keeps you on the edge of your seat. Some of my favorite books are Unwind Dystology and Crimson Bound. I have created this online portfolio as a project for AME and to also enhance my skills and understanding in website-making.
